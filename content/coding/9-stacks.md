Usage Details for C++
Declaration:  ```stack<int> myStack;```
Functions
*  Top element: myStack.top();
*  Push new element: myStack.push();
*  Pop top element: myStack.pop();
*  Check Empty: myStack.empty();
*  Size of the stack: myStack.size();
